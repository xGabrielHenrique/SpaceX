Falcon 9

Falcon 9 is a reusable two-stage rocket developed by SpaceX for orbital missions, featuring nine powerful Merlin engines and the ability to vertically land its first stage.

Falcon Heavy

The Falcon Heavy is a powerful, reusable rocket developed by SpaceX, consisting of three Falcon 9 rocket cores. It can carry heavy payloads to space and is capable of vertical landing.
